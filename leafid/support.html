Frequently asked questions

How do I identify a leaf?

Tap Take Photo (or Choose from Library), photograph a single leaf laid flat on a plain background in good light, and the app returns the top matching species within seconds — ranked by confidence, each with full details.

Why did I get a low-confidence warning?

The app tells you honestly when a match is uncertain rather than guessing confidently. Retake the photo with one leaf filling most of the frame, on a plain background, in daylight, and compare the listed candidates — including their lookalikes — before trusting a match.

Is the identification always correct?

No — identifications are AI-generated and can be wrong. Every result includes confidence scores, reference images, and verification links so you can confirm independently. Never eat, touch, or feed a plant to animals based on this app alone.

How does the free identification work?

Every new install includes one free, full identification (or an instant example scan) before any subscription is required — so you can see a real result first.

How do subscriptions and the free trial work?

Leaf ID Pro is available as $4.99/week with a 3-day free trial, or $29.99/year. Payment is charged to your Apple Account. Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the current period ends. You won't be charged if you cancel during the trial.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings → your Apple Account → Subscriptions → Leaf Identification and tap Cancel. Cancelling stops future charges; access continues until the end of the paid period. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

I subscribed but the app still shows the paywall.

Tap Restore Purchases on the paywall or in the app's Settings. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple Account used to subscribe. If it still doesn't unlock, email us.

Is there a limit to "unlimited" identifications?

Subscriptions include generous fair-use limits (currently 30 identifications per day) designed so that normal personal use never encounters them. They exist only to prevent automated abuse. Details are in the Terms of Use.

How do I delete my identification history?

Long-press any entry on the home screen and choose Delete, or open an entry and tap the trash icon. History is stored only on your device — deleting the app removes it entirely.

What happens to my photos?

Photos are sent securely to our identification service to produce the result, and your history is stored only on your device. There are no accounts, no ads, and no tracking. See the Privacy Policy for full details.

Report a problem

Found a bug or a wildly wrong identification? Email support@leanorbit.app with your device model, iOS version, and (if you can) the photo you scanned — it helps us improve the matching.

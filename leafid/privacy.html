Privacy Policy

Leaf Identification: Plant ID

Effective July 19, 2026

The short version: we don't want your data. There are no accounts, no ads, no analytics, and no tracking. Leaf photos you submit are used once — to identify the plant — and your identification history lives only on your device.

Who we are

Leaf Identification: Plant ID ("the app") is published by LeanOrbit ("we"). You can reach us anytime at support@leanorbit.app.

What the app processes, and why

Leaf photos. When you identify a leaf, the photo you take or choose is sent over an encrypted connection to our server, which forwards it to our botanical identification provider, Kindwise s.r.o. ("Plant.id"), to produce the identification result. Photos are used solely to generate that result. Our server temporarily retains a cryptographic fingerprint (hash) of the image and its identification result for up to 7 days so that rescanning the same photo returns instantly; the photo itself is not stored on our server. Plant.id processes images under its own privacy policy: kindwise.com/privacy-policy.

A per-install device identifier. The app sends Apple's "identifier for vendor" — a random ID unique to the app installation, not to you — with each identification request. We use it only to enforce fair-use limits and prevent abuse of the identification service. It is not linked to your identity and is not used for advertising or tracking. Usage counters attached to it are automatically deleted within 40 days.

Purchases. Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details, name, or Apple Account. Purchase state (whether a subscription is active) is verified on your device through Apple's StoreKit.

What stays on your device

  • Your identification history — photos, dates, and results — is stored only on your device. Deleting an entry, or the app, deletes it.
  • Your free-scan and onboarding status, stored in the device keychain.

What we don't do

  • No user accounts and no sign-up — we can't identify who you are.
  • No advertising, no analytics SDKs, no tracking of any kind.
  • No sale or sharing of personal information with third parties for their own purposes.
  • No collection of location data.

Service providers

We use three service providers, strictly to operate the app: Apple (App Store, payments), Cloudflare (our identification relay server), and Kindwise/Plant.id (botanical identification). Species imagery and descriptions shown in the app are sourced from public botanical databases including Wikipedia (CC BY-SA), with attribution links in the app.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. It contains no social features, no ads, and no data entry beyond choosing a photo.

Your choices and rights

  • You can deny camera access and use only library photos (or the built-in example).
  • You can delete your entire history at any time in the app, or by deleting the app.
  • Depending on where you live (e.g., GDPR or CCPA regions), you may have rights to access or delete data. Because we hold no account-linked data, most requests are satisfied by deleting the app — but email us at support@leanorbit.app and we will help with anything else.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will post the new version at this address with an updated effective date. Material changes will be highlighted in the app's release notes.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@leanorbit.app.

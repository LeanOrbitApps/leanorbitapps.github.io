See the apps Our philosophy

Three rules. We hold them like gravity.

Every feature, every line of code, every release decision passes through these three checks before it ships.

01 — Lean

Every feature earns its place.

No bloat. No upsells. No dark patterns. Apps that load fast, stay small, and respect the seconds of your day.

02 — Intelligent

AI shows up where it actually helps.

Quietly in the background — to summarize, surface, suggest. Never demanding your attention or pretending to be your friend.

03 — Private

Your data stays on your device.

We don't collect it, sell it, or send it anywhere. No accounts required. No analytics watching. No telling.

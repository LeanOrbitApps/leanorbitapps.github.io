Privacy Policy

Stockroom Inventory: ReStocker

Last updated: June 29, 2026

The short version: ReStocker keeps your data on your own device and in your private iCloud account. We don't require an account, we can't see your inventory, and we don't sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

This Privacy Policy explains how Stockroom Inventory: ReStocker ("ReStocker," "the app," "we," "us") handles information when you use the app. By using ReStocker, you agree to the practices described here.

Information You Provide

ReStocker is a personal inventory tool. The information you enter — including item names, quantities, SKUs, unit costs, par/reorder levels, locations, categories, notes, expiration dates, item photos, and barcode values — is created and controlled by you.

This information is stored locally on your device and, if you have iCloud enabled, synced to your own private iCloud (CloudKit) database. It syncs only across devices signed in to your personal Apple Account. We, the developer, do not have access to this data and never receive a copy of it.

No Account Required

ReStocker does not have its own login or user accounts. We do not ask for your name, email address, or password to use the app. Any syncing uses Apple's iCloud, which is tied to your existing Apple Account and governed by Apple's privacy practices.

Camera & Barcode Scanning

With your permission, ReStocker uses your device camera to scan barcodes and QR codes so you can add and count stock quickly. The camera feed is processed on your device to read codes. We do not record, store, or transmit the camera feed. A photo is only saved when you deliberately attach one to an item, and those photos are stored with your inventory data as described above.

If you use the optional product look-up feature, the scanned barcode number may be sent to a third-party product database to retrieve generic product details (such as a product name). This request contains only the barcode value and no personal information about you.

Import & Export

You can import inventory from a CSV file and export your data as CSV or text using the iOS share sheet. These actions happen on your device and are initiated entirely by you. Where the exported file goes (email, Files, another app, etc.) is your choice and is handled by iOS, not by us.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not collect your location, contacts, or device identifiers for advertising.
  • We do not sell or rent any data to anyone.

How Your Data Is Stored & Protected

Your inventory lives on your device and, optionally, in your private iCloud account. Data in iCloud is protected by Apple's security and encryption. You can learn more in Apple's Privacy Policy and iCloud security overview.

Data Retention & Deletion

Because your data is stored under your control, you can remove it at any time:

  • Delete individual items inside the app.
  • Delete the app to remove its local data from that device.
  • Manage or delete the app's iCloud data in Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage.

Children's Privacy

ReStocker is a business and productivity tool intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes may also be noted in the app or its App Store release notes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at support@leanorbit.com.
